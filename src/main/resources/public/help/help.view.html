Help Page (Here You Can Learn Things About Our Site)

Add a League

We understand that you Love Fantasy football and know you probably have more than one league. This is how you add a new league to your account.

Awards

Awards are great to get. Except for when you receive one for being the worst performer in your Fantasy League. The Awards page is where you can see all of the awards you received.

Draft Buddy

Draft Buddy is a great and useful tool for helping you at the beginning of your Fantasy season. It will help you by displaying players in order from highest to lowest values based on ESPN player rankings.

Fair Trade Judge

The Fair Trade Judge will perform an analysis of projected scores for the remaining weeks of the players involved in the trade. It is easy to use and will return you a ruling on the trade. (Extremely Equal, Fair, Unfair, Robbery)

Insult Generator

Thinking of interesting and funny insults add to the fun of the game. Let this insult generator create insults for you. By selecting the team you want to insult and pressing the button, it will automatically generate a personalized insult for that team.

League Poll

League Poll is used to liven your fantasy football experience with your league. By using this tool, it creates a poll which allows each team to input their ranks and runs our algorithm to produce the results.

Smart Rankings

Smart Rankings gives you a good gauge of where you stand and where you could improve your fantasy team. Smart Rankings Analyzs your team and all the other teams for a given league and calculates a score. It also informs you of projected strengths and weakness.

Waiver Wire Pickup Aid

This is a great tool that will give you a step up on your fellow Fantasy counterparts. This will run our complicated algorithm to help you select the best player to pick up for your team. Through an analysis of your team and possible players available, it will display who you should pick up off the wire.
