About

Fantasy Football Analyst's main goal is to help people improve their fantasy football experiences. We can analyze statistics to assist and infrom fantasy football owners. Every week users are faced with crucial and difficult choices that directly affect their teams outcomes.

This application will provide information and analysis to improve the strategic choices made by users. Whether you are wondering who to start/sit, analyze a trade, or find a better place to communicate with your league, this is the perfect place for you.
