Help Page (Here You Can Learn Things About Our Site)

Add a League

We understand that you Love Fantasy football and know you probably have more than one league. This is how you add a new leage to your account.

Awards

Awards are great to get. Except for when you recieve one for being the worst performer in your Fantasy League. The Awards page is where you can see all of the awards you recieved.

Draft Buddy

Draft Buddy is a great and useful tool for helping you at the beginning of your Fanstasy season. It will help you by displaying palyers in order from highest to lowest values based on ESPN palyer rankings.

Fair Trade Judge

The Fair Trade Judeg will perform an analysis of projected scores for the remaining weeks of the players involved in the trade. It is easy to use and will retun you a rulling on the trade. (Extreamly Equal, Fair, Unfair, Robbery)

Insult Generator

Thinking of interesing and funny insults add to the fun of the game. Let this instult generator create insults for you. By selecting the team you want to instult and pressing the button, it will automatically generate a personalized insult for that team.

League Poll

League Poll is used to liven your fantasy football experience with your league. By using this tool, it creates a poll which allows each team to input thier ranks and runs our algorithm to produce the results.

Smart Rankings

Smart Rankings gives you a good gauge of where you stand and where you could improve your fantasy team. Smart Rankings Analyses your team and all the other teams for a given league and cacluates a score. It also informs you of projected strengths and weakness.

Waiver Wire Pickup Aid

This is a great tool that will give you a step up on your fellow Fantasy counterparts. This will run our complicated algorithm to help you select the best player to pick up for your team. Through an analysis of your team and possible players available, it will display who you should pick up off the wire.
